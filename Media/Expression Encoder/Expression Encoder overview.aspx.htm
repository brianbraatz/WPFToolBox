Expression Encoder

Publish Silverlight Experiences

Microsoft® Expression® Encoder, a feature of Expression Media, offers encoding, enhancement, and publishing of rich media experiences to Microsoft Silverlight.

  • Free Trial Download
  • Buy Expression Encoder
 

Encoding for Everyone

 Expression Encoder's accessible, friendly user interface gives designers, editors and producers the ability to generate gorgeous VC-1-encoded content wrapped in a Silverlight experience. The same encoding engine can be controlled via command line for compressionists churning through high-volume, repetitive encoding chores.

Make Your Mark

 Add leaders and trailers to your media files to advertise or roll credits. Watermark your video with your corporate ID. Create custom job presets to ensure consistent branding and media quality. Include metadata, create chapter navigation and embed captions to provide a targeted media experiences.

Fastest Path to Silverlight

 Delivering a Web-ready Silverlight experience is easy. Import QuickTime®, AVI, MPEG, WMV, and additional media formats into Expression Encoder, fine-tune your VC-1 settings, and choose your favorite Silverlight template.
