New WPF/E Video Player

January 23rd 2007 Posted to Examples, XAML, Video, WPF/E

I just finished off a new WPF/E video player that I plan to add to contentpresenter.com. It includes a rewind button, a play/pause toggle button, and a mute toggle button. There is a grey bar that shows the loading progress and a blue bar which shows the playhead position. There is also a timecode readout. You can check it out here and the full source is avaialble here.
