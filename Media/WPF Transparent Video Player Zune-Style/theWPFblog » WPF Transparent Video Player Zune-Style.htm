WPF Transparent Video Player: Zune-Style

October 05th 2006 Posted to Examples, XAML, Video, Sparkle

Check out one of the projects that was built during last night’s WPF After Hours session. It was designed by Matthew Bice who is amazing at creating slick look interfaces. This was based off of the new Zune device that will be coming out soon. I took Matthew’s designs and created a basic video player with a transparent window. You can drag it around your desktop using the rubber bar at the top of the player. Right now it only plays one video which is included in the Zip file and the controls are pretty basic. Since we only had a short period of time to do this, usuability wasn’t the first thing on our mind . Click on the image below to download the Zip file and make sure the video file is in the same folder as the EXE file.
