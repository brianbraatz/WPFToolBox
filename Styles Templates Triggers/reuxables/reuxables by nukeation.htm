What are reuxables™?

Reuxables are a whole new range of tools, widgets, and resources for Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF), Microsoft Visual Studio 2008 (Codename "ORCAS") and Microsoft Expression Blend. The first offering of the reuxables line of products will be the reuxables THEMES.

What are reuxables THEMES?

Reuxables THEMES are WPF application themes (ResourceDictionaries) that when dropped into your project give your application a whole new custom look!

What is the difference between EXPRESS and PROFESSIONAL editions?

We plan to release most THEMES in both EXPRESS and PROFESSIONAL editions.

EXPRESS editions are fixed, compiled DLLs that you add to your projects as a reference. You can't tweak or edit the themes in any way.

PROFESSIONAL editions are fully customizable. In fact, they come with a reuxables Theme Generator which lets you edit the theme in over 16 million ways! If that's not enough, you can take the generated source code (yes, the PROFESSIONAL edition gives you full XAML code) and edit it however you like.

How much will reuxables THEMES cost?

EXPRESS themes will cost somewhere between $49 to $99.

PROFESSIONAL themes will cost somewhere between $149 to $399.

What software do I need to use reuxables THEMES?

Microsoft Expression Blend 1.0 or later, or Microsoft Visual Studio 2008 ("ORCAS") Beta or later.

When will reuxables THEMES be available?

THEMES will be released by Q3 2007, followed shortly by our many other offerings. Stay tuned to this site for more information.
