MatrixAnimationUsingPath Demo

May 16, 2006
New York City

He's half man, half unicycle, and he looks ridiculous as he travels over the hills and valleys of Bezier Boulevard, wheel spinning, arms waving for balance, unknowingly demonstrating the MatrixAnimationUsingPath class with the DoesRotateWithTangent property set to true.

It's all here in the UnicycleMan.xaml file, one of the sample programs in my 75-page chapter on WPF animation.
