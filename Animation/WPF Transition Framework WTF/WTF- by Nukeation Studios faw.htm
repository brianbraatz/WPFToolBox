How do I use WTF?
Using WTF is simple. WTF is like any other container control (Grid, StackPanel, etc.). All you do is create WTF control, and put your controls that you want transitioned inside it. Set the duration, quality, and transition type properties and you're done.

How do I use multiple transitions?
Put a WTF object inside another WTF object. Set both to different transitions of your choice. Fire them simultaneously and you will achieve multiple transitions. There is no limit (outside of obvious memory and hardware limits) to how many transitions you can nest.

Can I use this in my commercial products?
Yes, as long as you stay within the limits of the EULA (license.txt supplied with the software) you can use WTF is your commerical products. Basically, you can't claim it to be your own and sell it or redistribute it under you own name. You can't reverse engineer or hack the code. And you have to include the LICENSE.txt file along with the WTFX.dll file.

Transitions are jerky or slow! What do I do?
Change your QUALITY setting to FASTER from BETTER (Default). This will lower the quality of the effects but increase the performance.
