What the...


Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF) provides the best tools and features to create the most compelling User Experience (UX) for your application. However, there is a lack of out-of-the-box support for transitions. WTF or the WPF Transition Framework is the solution.

WTF provides easy XAML based access to over 8 most basic and useful transitions including blur, fade, slide, and zoom. WTF is fully compatible with any WPF control - including custom and 3rd party controls. You can use unlimited WTFs in your app - and you get complete controls on the transitions whether they're timed or triggered. Oh, and WTF is free!!

Using WTF is extremely simple. In fact, we promise you that just looking at the code below will explain it all to you. If you still have questions, don't forget the FAQ.

WTF in Action:

<WTFX:WTF Duration="250" Transition="BlurOut" Quality="Better">
        <!-- Put your stuff here --> 
</WTFX:WTF>
