Have you tried using a Treeview control with a custom template for the TreeViewItems. In stead of using an ItemsPresenter you can use a ComboBox. To display the 'next' item in the path, use a ContentPresenter (or something similar that renders the next TreeViewItem) and link it to the the 'SelectedItem'. Best to take a look at the Template of the TreeViewItem. Probably, the ItemsPanel should be changed into a Horizontal StackPanel.
