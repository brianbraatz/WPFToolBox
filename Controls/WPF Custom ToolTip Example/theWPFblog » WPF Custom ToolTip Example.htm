WPF Custom ToolTip Example

October 21st 2006 Posted to Examples, XAML

I was on a plane last night and decided to pull out the laptop for some quick WPF action. I wanted to see how easy it was to customize the tooltips that popup everywhere on most applications. Luckily, the ToolTip control is just as easy to customize as any other type of control. See the example below where I turned it into a more Web 2.0 style of tooltip. It's big, bubbly, and totally over the top. Maybe my design skills are affected by altitude . You can click on the image to see the example and the XAML style is below that.

       

PLAIN TEXT
XML:
  1. <Style TargetType="{x:Type ToolTip}">
  2.     <Setter Property="OverridesDefaultStyle" Value="true"/>
  3.     <Setter Property="VerticalOffset" Value="-2" />
  4.     <Setter Property="HorizontalOffset" Value="20" />
  5.     <Setter Property="Placement" Value="Top" />
  6.     <Setter Property="Template">
  7.         <Setter.Value>
  8.             <ControlTemplate TargetType="{x:Type ToolTip}">
  9.                 <Grid Name="Border" Background="Transparent" Width="{TemplateBinding Width}" Height="60">
  10.                     <Rectangle Height="35" RadiusX="7.5" RadiusY="7.5">
  11.                         <Rectangle.Fill>
  12.                             <LinearGradientBrush StartPoint="0.5,-0.5" EndPoint="0.547,0.913">
  13.                                 <GradientStop Color="#FFEEEEEE" Offset="0"/>
  14.                                 <GradientStop Color="#FFBBBBBB" Offset="1"/>
  15.                             </LinearGradientBrush>
  16.                         </Rectangle.Fill>
  17.                     </Rectangle>
  18.                     <Path Fill="#FFBBBBBB" Stretch="Fill" HorizontalAlignment="Left" Margin="10,45,0,0" Width="15.692" Height="14.81" Data="M130,122 L138,136 144,122"/>
  19.                     <ContentPresenter Margin="10,0,10,0" HorizontalAlignment="Center" VerticalAlignment="Center" TextBlock.Foreground="Black" TextBlock.FontSize="12" />
  20.                 </Grid>
  21.             </ControlTemplate>
  22.         </Setter.Value>
  23.     </Setter>
  24. </Style>
