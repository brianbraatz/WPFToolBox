An Analog Clock

Here is an analog clock application for Windows Presentation Foundation created with Expression Interactive Designer and Visual C# Express Edition. To use this on WindowsXP you need some stuff from this Get The Beta page.

 

Larger View

This clock was a lot of fun to make. I spent much more time in Expression Graphic Designer than I have with previous projects, both in creating the multi-path vectors for the outer frame of the clock, and also experimenting with different overall designs.

This clock, and just about any analog design, would benefit greatly by some transparency through to the desktop--getting rid of the black square, essentially. Although Unni Ravindranathan has a great sample Stock Watch Widget demonstrating a technique to achieve transparency, I have yet to figure out how to make such applications draggable, much less resizable (love that vector scaling!). I have a couple ideas for approaches to this, but won't be able to get to them for a week or so. Maybe someone else will have posted a method by then.
