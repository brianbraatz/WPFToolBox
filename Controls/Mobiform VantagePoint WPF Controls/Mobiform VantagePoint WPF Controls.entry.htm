Mobiform: VantagePoint WPF Controls

Another control pack for WPF - VantagePoint WPF Controls, this time from Mobiform Software, who were early adopters of WPF for their Aurora Xaml Designer.  This set of controls looks different than most I've look at:

VantagePoint consists of Buttons, Tanks, Pipes, Sliders, Radial and Linear Gauges, Motors, and other interface controls.

It seems like the Tanks, Pipes, Slides, Gauges and Motors is a way to visualize (and control) real world devices, factories, etc...
