Slinky XAML ListBox

June 22nd 2006 Posted to Examples, XAML


I'm currently in Austin TX working on a large-scale WPF project. I can't say what the project is or even who the client is for that matter, but it is giving me some great experience using XAML. As you can tell from my previous posts I have a fondness for C# and have avoided learning XAML. After using it for a few days I'm definitely getting a better grip on WPF as a whole.

Below is my first pure-XAML creation. It is a ListBox control that has been completely redesigned and has a nice slinky rollover animation. I didn't style the scrollbar as for something like this I would rather increase the height of the ListBox to fill all the items. This is something that could be used for a navigation menu. Click on the image below to check it out and I've also put the XAML below that.

PLAIN TEXT
XML:
  1. <Grid
  2.     xmlns="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml/presentation"
  3.     xmlns:x="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml"
  4.     xmlns:mc="http://schemas.openxmlformats.org/markup-compatibility/2006"
  5.     xmlns:d="http://schemas.microsoft.com/expression/interactivedesigner/2006"
  6.     mc:Ignorable="d"
  7.     Background="#000000"
  8.     x:Name="DocumentRoot"
  9.     x:Class="UntitledProject1.Scene1"
  10.     Width="734" Height="547">
  11.  
  12.     <Grid.Resources>
  13.         <Style x:Key="{x:Type ListBox}" TargetType="{x:Type ListBox}">
  14.         <Setter Property="Cursor" Value="Hand" />
  15.             <Setter Property="Template">
  16.                 <Setter.Value>
  17.                     <ControlTemplate>
  18.                         <Border>
  19.                             <ScrollViewer>
  20.                                 <ItemsPresenter />
  21.                             </ScrollViewer>
  22.                         </Border>
  23.                     </ControlTemplate>
  24.                 </Setter.Value>
  25.             </Setter>
  26.         </Style>
  27.        
  28.         <Style x:Key="{x:Type ListBoxItem}" TargetType="{x:Type ListBoxItem}">
  29.             <Setter Property="Foreground" Value="#FFFFFF" />
  30.             <Setter Property="FontFamily" Value="Verdana" />
  31.             <Setter Property="FontSize" Value="11" />
  32.             <Setter Property="Template">
  33.                 <Setter.Value>
  34.                     <ControlTemplate TargetType="{x:Type ListBoxItem}">
  35.                         <Canvas Width="Auto" Height="25">
  36.                             <Rectangle Name="filler" Canvas.Top="0" Canvas.Left="0" Width="200" Height="25">
  37.                                 <Rectangle.Fill>
  38.                                     <SolidColorBrush x:Name="fillb" Color="#333333" />
  39.                                 </Rectangle.Fill>
  40.                             </Rectangle>
  41.                             <Path d:LastTangent="0,0" Stroke="{x:Null}" Fill="#FF6E00" HorizontalAlignment="Right" VerticalAlignment="Top" Width="7" Height="7" Canvas.Left="15" Opacity="0" Canvas.Top="6" x:Name="Path" RenderTransformOrigin="0.5,0.5" Stretch="Fill" Data="M601.11544,190.39485 L590.06202,213.0964 613,213">
  42.                                 <Path.RenderTransform>
  43.                                     <TransformGroup>
  44.                                         <RotateTransform Angle="90"/>
  45.                                     </TransformGroup>
  46.                                 </Path.RenderTransform>
  47.                             </Path>
  48.                             <ContentPresenter Name="con" Canvas.Top="2" Canvas.Left="10" />
  49.                         </Canvas>
  50.                          <ControlTemplate.Triggers>
  51.                                     <EventTrigger RoutedEvent="Button.MouseEnter">
  52.                           <BeginStoryboard>
  53.                                             <Storyboard>
  54.                                   <ColorAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="fillb" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(SolidColorBrush.Color)" From="#333333" To="#111111" Duration="0:0:0.1" />   
  55.                                   <DoubleAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="con" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(Canvas.Left)" From="10" To="30" Duration="0:0:0.1" />
  56.                                   <DoubleAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="Path" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(Path.Opacity)" From="0" To="1" Duration="0:0:0.1" />
  57.                                   <DoubleAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="Path" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(Canvas.Left)" From="15" To="10" Duration="0:0:0.2" />
  58.                                             </Storyboard>
  59.                           </BeginStoryboard>
  60.                  </EventTrigger>
  61.                                     <EventTrigger RoutedEvent="Button.MouseLeave">
  62.                           <BeginStoryboard>
  63.                                         <Storyboard>
  64.                                   <ColorAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="fillb" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(SolidColorBrush.Color)" From="#111111" To="#333333" Duration="0:0:0.8" />
  65.                                   <DoubleAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="con" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(Canvas.Left)" From="30" To="10" Duration="0:0:0.3" />
  66.                                   <DoubleAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="Path" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(Path.Opacity)" From="1" To="0" Duration="0:0:0.1" />
  67.                                   <DoubleAnimation Storyboard.TargetName="Path" Storyboard.TargetProperty="(Canvas.Left)" From="10" To="15" Duration="0:0:0.1" />
  68.                                             </Storyboard>
  69.                           </BeginStoryboard>
  70.                                     </EventTrigger>
  71.                                 </ControlTemplate.Triggers>
  72.                     </ControlTemplate>
  73.                 </Setter.Value>
  74.             </Setter>
  75.         </Style>
  76.        
  77.     </Grid.Resources>
